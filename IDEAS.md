  Gameplay Mechanics:                          
  - Ramming bonus — extra damage/score for hitting cars from behind or head-on vs
  side-swipes                                                                           
  - Weight classes — let player pick light (fast, fragile), medium, or heavy (slow,
  tanky) car at start                                                                   
  - Arena hazards — oil slicks that make you spin, fire patches that deal damage over 
  time, crushers/pistons that slam periodically                                       
  - Shrinking arena — walls slowly close in over time, forcing tighter fights
  - Waves/rounds — instead of one big fight, waves of enemies with short breaks between,
   getting harder each round
  - Boss cars — every few waves a big armored truck appears with more health and special
   attacks

  Power-ups & Items:
  - Mines — drop behind your car, explode when someone drives over them
  - EMP blast — stuns nearby cars for 1-2 seconds
  - Ram shield — temporary front-facing battering ram that does massive damage
  - Repair drone — slowly heals you over time
  - Ghost mode — become transparent and pass through cars briefly
  - Homing missile — locks onto nearest enemy

  Progression & Meta:
  - Unlock system — earn coins per match, unlock new car bodies/colors/spoilers
  - Upgrades — spend coins on armor, speed, nitro capacity between matches
  - Leaderboard — local high score table saved in localStorage
  - Achievements — "First Blood", "10 kills in one match", "Win without dying", etc.
  - Car customization — pick body style, stripe pattern, roof decal at start screen

  Visual & Feel:
  - Screen shake on big hits
  - Slow-motion on kills (brief 0.3s slowdown when you wreck someone)
  - Tire tracks that persist longer and look more realistic
  - Day/night cycle — arena gradually darkens, headlights become visible
  - Weather — rain with puddles (slippery), dust storms (reduced visibility)
  - Replay camera — after game over, show the best kill from a cinematic angle

  Multiplayer Enhancements:
  - Team mode — 2v2 with AI teammates
  - King of the Hill — score by staying in a glowing zone in the center
  - Capture the flag — grab a flag and bring it to your zone
  - Tag mode — one car is "it", scores points by tagging others

  Audio:
  - Background music — synthwave or chiptune loop (toggleable)
  - Announcer voice — synthesized speech for "Double kill!", "Rampage!", etc.
  - Different impact sounds based on speed/angle

  Quality of Life:
  - Settings panel — adjust SFX volume, number of AI, difficulty
  - Difficulty levels — easy (dumb AI, less damage), hard (smart AI, aggressive)
  - Tutorial — first-time animated arrows showing controls
  - Mobile touch controls — virtual joystick + buttons for phone/tablet play
